About Zayna

Preserving tradition through authentic Moroccan craftsmanship

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Zayna began with a simple mission: to bring the vibrant artistry of Moroccan craftsmanship to the world while supporting local artisans and preserving traditional techniques.

We work directly with over 200 skilled craftspeople across Morocco, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable production methods. Each piece in our collection tells a story of cultural heritage, passed down through generations.

From the intricate patterns of Fez ceramics to the rich textures of Berber rugs, every product embodies centuries of artistic tradition. We invite you to bring a piece of Morocco's vibrant culture into your home.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Discover the meticulous process behind our handcrafted products

Selection of Materials

Our artisans carefully select the finest raw materials, from high-quality wool for rugs to locally sourced clay for pottery. Each material is chosen for its durability, texture, and aesthetic quality.

Traditional Design

Patterns and designs are drawn from centuries of Moroccan artistic tradition. Many motifs have symbolic meaning, telling stories of cultural beliefs, protection, and prosperity.

Skilled Crafting

Using techniques passed down through generations, our artisans transform raw materials into beautiful pieces. This process often takes days or even weeks, requiring patience and exceptional skill.

Finishing Touches

Each piece receives careful finishing to ensure durability and beauty. This may include natural dyeing processes, polishing, or detailing that brings the creation to life with vibrant colors and textures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How can I determine the authenticity of Moroccan handicrafts?

Authentic Moroccan handicrafts are characterized by handmade quality, natural materials, traditional designs, and irregular patterns that show human creation rather than machine precision. Each Zayna product comes with an authenticity certificate detailing its origin, materials, and the artisan who created it.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We offer standard shipping (10-15 business days) and express shipping (3-5 business days) options for international orders. For orders within Morocco, delivery typically takes 1-3 business days. All items are carefully packaged to ensure safe arrival.

How do I care for my Moroccan rug?

For regular maintenance, vacuum your rug using a low-power setting without beater bars. For spills, blot immediately with a clean cloth. Professional cleaning is recommended every 1-2 years. Rotate your rug periodically to ensure even wear, and keep out of direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Can I request custom sizes or colors for products?

Yes! We offer custom orders for most of our products. You can specify dimensions, colors, and even design elements. Custom orders typically take 4-8 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items in their original condition. Custom orders are non-returnable. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect. Please contact customer service to initiate a return.
